Welcome to Caml Capital!

Players: A minimum of 2 players is required to start the game. If you wish to omit additional players during setup, simply press Enter when prompted.

Rolling the Dice: To roll the dice and advance on the board, press Enter. Your fate lies just a roll away!

Buying Properties: Interested in a property you've landed on? Type "BUY" to make it yours. Wise investments can lead to victory!

Game Objective: Be cautious with your finances! The game ends when a player goes bankrupt. Aim to be the last mogul standing to win.

Dev Mode: For developers and cheaters alike! This mode allows the players to choose which position they want to move to. Enter it by typing "DEV" in
start screen!

Best of luck in your real estate endeavors in Caml Capital!
